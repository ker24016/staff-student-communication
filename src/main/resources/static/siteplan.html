Content

Home page

There are some really wild creatures out there, but if you thought you already knew about all of their features, think again! This website houses a collection of some of the most wild and wooly fun facts about animals! For example, did you know that geckos use their tongues to clean their eyes instead of blinking? Or that no pattern of zebra stripes is ever the same, and is always completely unique? Join us as we find out about some of these creatures!

Images for the Home page

Peregrine Falcon

The Peregrine Falcon is one of the worlds greatest wonders! It is literally the fastest animal in the world! It can soar in heights up to 3500 feet before swooping down at over 200 miles per hour to catch their prey. If you thought that your car was fast, just wait until you see this animal!

Images for the Page 2

Red Panda

The Red Panda is not your average panda, it's genetics are more like a racoon than anything else! A Red Panda eats bamboo for most of it's life, but is also a carnivore. While it is not related to the Giant Panda(the black and white kind), it is called the Original Panda because it was the first animal to be called panda and gain its fame.
